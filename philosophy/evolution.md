# Evolution & Contradictions

## Major Shifts

### Political Philosophy (10+ years ago → now)

**Before:** Considered myself a Republican. Traditional conservative worldview.

**After:** Full anarcho-capitalist. NAP (Non-Aggression Principle) as the foundation for how things should work, regardless of government.

**Catalyst:** Bitcoin. Finding Bitcoin led to questioning everything about government, institutions, money, and control. A profound change in views on everything.

### Training Intensity (Past → now)

**Before:** 100% intensity every day. Go hard every session.

**After:** Consistency over intensity. Rarely above 50% in training. Training is for technique, not winning.

**Catalyst:** Injuries. Got hurt enough times to learn the value of sustainable training.

## What Stays Constant

Despite evolution in specific beliefs:
- Sovereignty has always mattered
- Action over theory
- Learning by doing
- Skepticism of institutions
- Building real things

## How I Update

I update when reality disagrees with me. The best minds say "I was wrong" and "this surprised me."

I'm aligned with truth, not ego. When something breaks my model, I change the model.
